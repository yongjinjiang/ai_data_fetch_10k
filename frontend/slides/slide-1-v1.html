Algorithm

  • Parse 10-K HTML tables with BeautifulSoup.
  • Regex keyword matching on row labels.
  • Pick first numeric cell as extracted value.

Key Result

Exact Accuracy: 73.3% (22/30) · MAPE: 7.78%

Main failure mode: revenue labels varied by company/industry, causing wrong subtotal picks or misses.

Takeaway

Simple rules work for stable templates, but brittle under label drift and table-format variation.
