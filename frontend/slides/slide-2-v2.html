Slide 2 · V2 Error-Driven Rule Refinement

Algorithm Upgrades

  • Anchored regex patterns (^...$) to avoid partial/sub-line matches.
  • Industry-specific synonyms (e.g., bank and energy style labels).
  • Symbol/per-share filtering to avoid noisy small values.
  • Priority ordering of patterns (specific first, generic later).

Key Result

Exact Accuracy: 90.0% (27/30) · MAPE: 3.48%

Improved substantially, but still struggled where semantic table context (units/column meaning) was needed.

Takeaway

Rule quality can be pushed far with careful error analysis, but maintenance cost grows quickly.
