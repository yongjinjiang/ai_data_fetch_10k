Slide 4.2 · Why This New Field + How GT Was Built

Why choose net_cash_from_operating_activities?

  • Expands from income/balance statements into cash flow statement logic.
  • Harder semantics: operating vs investing/financing, sign handling, period alignment.
  • More realistic for production extraction robustness than only revenue/assets.

Ground Truth Workflow (Fast + Reliable)

  1. Use the strongest model (GPT-5.4) to generate initial number suggestions.
  2. Manually validate each company against cached 10-K HTML tables/tags.
  3. Correct mismatches and finalize into ground_truth.json.

Principle: AI accelerates candidate generation; human validation guarantees final label quality.

Interview Message

I intentionally increased problem difficulty with a new field, then used AI-assisted labeling + manual verification to keep data quality high and iteration speed fast.
